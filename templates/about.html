Why ScALe Matters

Precision in plant research requires accurate measurements. ScALe (Seed Counter And Leaf Evaluator) provides researchers with reliable digital tools for seed counting and leaf analysis.

How It Helps in Research

ScALe eliminates manual counting and measurement errors, saving hours of tedious work. Our image processing algorithms deliver consistent, reproducible results for your agricultural studies.

Available Tools

  • Seed Counter: Automated counting of rice, maize, or millet seeds
  • Leaf Evaluator: Precise measurement of leaf area, length, and width

How ScALe Works

  1. Prepare your sample following our imaging guidelines
  2. Upload the image to ScALe
  3. Select the appropriate analysis tool
  4. Receive instant, accurate measurements
